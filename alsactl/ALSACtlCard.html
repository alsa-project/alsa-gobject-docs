The “handle-disconnection” signal

void
user_function (ALSACtlCard *self,
               gpointer     user_data)

When the sound card is not available anymore due to unbinding driver or hot unplugging, this signal is emit. The owner of this object should call g_object_free() as quickly as possible to release ALSA control character device.
